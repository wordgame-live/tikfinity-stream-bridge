Quick help & tips

What does this app do?
It connects to TikFinity on your computer and forwards TikTok chat events to Word Tycoon game pages. Keep it running while you stream.

How do I use it with OBS?
Add a Browser Source in OBS and set the URL to one of the game stream pages above (e.g. https://rectangled.live/stream). The game will automatically connect to this bridge.

What is 127.0.0.1:21420?
That's the local address this bridge runs on. It's only accessible on your computer — nothing is sent to the internet.
